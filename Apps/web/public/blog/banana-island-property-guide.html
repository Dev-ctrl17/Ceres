Why Banana Island?

Banana Island offers 24/7 private security, dedicated power supply, treated water, private beach access, a golf course, and some of the most spectacular waterfront views in Lagos. It's home to Nigeria's wealthiest individuals, diplomats, and top executives. Property values appreciate at 25-35% annually.

Investment Potential

Banana Island offers the strongest capital appreciation of any Nigerian real estate market. With only 400 plots and extreme scarcity, prices have risen consistently. Rental yields range from 6-8% for apartments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Banana Island better than Ikoyi?

Banana Island offers more exclusivity, better security, and waterfront living. Ikoyi offers more convenience for daily business. Banana Island is 30-50% more expensive per square meter.

Can I rent on Banana Island?

Yes, Banana Island has rental properties starting from ₦12 million annually for apartments to ₦50 million+ for waterfront mansions.
