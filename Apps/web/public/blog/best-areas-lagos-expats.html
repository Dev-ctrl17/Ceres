Victoria Island — The Expat Hub

Victoria Island is the most popular area for expats in Lagos. It's home to embassies, international restaurants, luxury hotels, and the best business infrastructure. Rent for a 3-bedroom luxury apartment ranges from ₦6-18 million per year.

Ikoyi — The Prestige Address

Ikoyi offers the most prestigious address in Lagos. It's quieter than Victoria Island with larger properties and better security. Ideal for families and executives who prefer a more residential environment.

Lekki Phase 1 — The Modern Choice

Lekki Phase 1 is popular with younger expats and families. It offers modern estates, better road networks, and proximity to international schools. It's more affordable than Ikoyi and Victoria Island.
