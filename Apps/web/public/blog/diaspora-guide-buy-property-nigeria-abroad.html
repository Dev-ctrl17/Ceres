Diaspora Guide: How to Buy Luxury Property in Nigeria from Abroad 2026

Buying luxury property in Nigeria from abroad is easier than ever. With modern PropTech solutions, secure digital transactions, and specialized agencies, diaspora Nigerians and foreign investors can purchase premium real estate without ever stepping foot in the country. This guide explains exactly how.

Step 1: Choose a Trusted Luxury Real Estate Agency

Partner with an agency offering virtual services. Luxury Properties Ltd provides live video tours, digital document verification, and secure remote payment systems for international buyers.

Step 2: Virtual Property Tour

Schedule a live video walkthrough. Request detailed photos, floor plans, neighborhood videos, and an independent structural engineer's report.

Step 3: Remote Document Verification

The agency's legal team verifies the Certificate of Occupancy (C of O) at the state land registry. You receive digital copies of all verified documents via secure portal.

Step 4: Digital Power of Attorney

Execute a Power of Attorney at the Nigerian embassy in your country to authorize a local representative to sign documents on your behalf.

Step 5: Secure Payment via Escrow

Transfer funds through verified international banking channels to an escrow account. Never send money to personal accounts.

Step 6: Receive Your Documents

After Governor's Consent, receive scanned legal documents digitally. Originals can be couriered to your international address.
