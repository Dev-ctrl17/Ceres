How to Verify Property Documents

Always verify documents at the state land registry. Your lawyer should check for encumbrances, liens, and government acquisition. This process takes 5-14 business days and costs ₦50,000-₦200,000.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Never buy property without verifying the C of O. Never pay cash without a receipt. Never sign documents without a lawyer present. Always use escrow for payments.
