How Long Does Governor's Consent Take? Timeline Guide 2026

Governor's Consent is the final legal step in transferring property ownership in Nigeria. This guide covers the exact timeline, costs, and how to speed up the process.

Governor's Consent Timeline (2026)

StageDurationWhat Happens
Document Preparation1-2 weeksGather all required documents
Filing Application1-2 daysSubmit to land registry
Initial Review2-4 weeksRegistry verifies documents
Governor's Approval2-6 weeksGovernor signs consent
Documentation1-2 weeksFinal documents prepared

Total Timeline: 6-12 weeks

The total processing time for Governor's Consent in Lagos is 6-12 weeks. However, with complete documents and an experienced lawyer, it can be as fast as 4-6 weeks.

Costs Breakdown

Consent Fee: ~10% of assessed value. Legal Fees: ₦100K-₦500K. Filing Fees: ₦50K-₦100K. Survey Fees: ₦100K-₦300K. For a ₦200 million property, expect total costs of ₦20-25 million.

Tips to Speed Up Processing

1. Ensure all documents are complete before filing. 2. Use an experienced property lawyer. 3. Follow up regularly at the land registry. 4. Consider expedited processing through your agency.
