What Affects Luxury Home Prices in Lagos?

Key factors include: location (waterfront vs inland), property size, number of bedrooms, security features, power supply, proximity to business districts, and the prestige of the neighborhood. Land scarcity in prime areas drives prices up significantly.

Hidden Costs to Budget For

Beyond the purchase price, budget 15-20% for: legal fees (5-10%), agency commission (5%), survey costs, Governor's Consent fees (10% of property value), and maintenance costs. For a ₦200 million property, expect ₦30-40 million in additional costs.
