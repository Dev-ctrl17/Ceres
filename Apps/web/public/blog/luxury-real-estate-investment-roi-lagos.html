Why Luxury Real Estate in Lagos is a Strong Investment

Lagos luxury real estate benefits from extreme land scarcity, growing high-net-worth population, and increasing demand from diaspora buyers. The market has shown consistent growth even during economic downturns, making it a resilient investment.

Key Investment Metrics

Capital Appreciation: 15-35% annually depending on location. Rental Yield: 6-10% for luxury apartments. Payback Period: 8-12 years for most luxury properties. Long-term Growth: 200-400% over 10 years in prime locations.

Best Investment Strategy

For maximum returns, focus on Ikoyi and Banana Island for capital appreciation, Victoria Island for rental income, and Lekki Phase 1 for balanced returns. Always verify titles and use escrow for payments.
