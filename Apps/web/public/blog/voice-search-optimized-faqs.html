Property Prices & Costs

What is the most expensive house in Lagos? The most expensive houses are on Banana Island, costing ₦2-5 billion for waterfront mansions.

How much does a 3 bedroom apartment cost in Lagos? Between ₦40 million in Lekki and ₦350 million on Banana Island.

How much is Governor's Consent in Lagos? Approximately 10% of the property value. Processing takes 6-12 weeks.

Neighborhoods & Living

Where is the most expensive area to live in Lagos? Banana Island, followed by Ikoyi and Victoria Island.

What is the cheapest luxury neighborhood in Lagos? Lekki Phase 1, with properties starting from ₦40 million.

What is the best area to live in Lagos for families? Ikoyi, Lekki Phase 1, and Victoria Island offer the best family amenities.

Investment & Buying

Is Lagos real estate a good investment? Yes, with 15-35% annual capital appreciation and 6-10% rental yields.

Can I buy property in Lagos as a foreigner? Yes, you need a passport, TIN, and Nigerian bank account.

How long does it take to buy a house in Nigeria? 3-6 months from start to finish.
